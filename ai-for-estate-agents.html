What is AI for Estate Agents?

AI for estate agents is artificial intelligence software designed to automate the repetitive tasks that consume most of an agent's day. Instead of manually responding to every enquiry, scheduling each viewing, and chasing references, AI handles these tasks automatically.

For UK estate agents and letting agents, this means:

  • Instant enquiry responses - AI responds to landlord and tenant enquiries within seconds, 24 hours a day
  • Automated lead qualification - AI scores leads based on value and urgency so you focus on the best opportunities
  • Smart viewing scheduling - Coordinate viewings automatically without back-and-forth emails
  • CRM integration - Works with your existing estate agent CRM and property portals
