What Makes the Best Estate Agent CRM?

The best CRM for real estate agents isn't just a database of contacts. It's a system that actively helps you win more business by automating the tasks that slow you down.

A great estate agent CRM should:

  • Capture leads automatically - Pull enquiries from Rightmove, Zoopla, OnTheMarket, and your website without manual entry
  • Follow up instantly - Respond to landlord enquiries within seconds, not hours
  • Score and prioritise leads - Know which landlords are most valuable and focus your time accordingly
  • Schedule viewings - Coordinate availability without endless back-and-forth emails
  • Track the entire journey - From first enquiry to move-in, see every interaction in one place

Lettings Quest adds AI automation to all of these features, so your CRM actually does the work instead of just storing data.
